HoloBaumark - Master project





This project was created for the Master Project, a course in my master studies that is two semesters long and the whole point of it is doing a "bigger" project. We were five people on the team and 2 1/2, me included, were programming. The other two did the design part.
The idea is that hardware stores can lend HoloLenses to customers who want to renovate their home in order to check out how the final product, like the flooring, will look like in the end.
This project won the first place in the "Mensch und Computer" Usablility Challenge 2018, category Master.
