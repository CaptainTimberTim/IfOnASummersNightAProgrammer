Bicycle Simulator - Winner: Best Prototype





This bicycle simulation was created during the Hackathon of the VR-Festival Places_. It won the "Best Prototype" which was the first place. The Hackathon was a 24h programming marathon, where a small team of 2-5 people creates a VR prototype based on small pitches given at the beginning. Here is the official website:
Places_ Hackathon (german)

Our prototype was created in Unity for the HTC Vive. It depicts 3 different scenarios in cities, the first two being awful for the biker and the last with a positiv example. The idea was to create an experience city planner or other policymaker can try out and see how unsafe it is cycling in cities, even when they don't do it themselves in real life.

This prototype was done in cooperation with Franziska Rücker and Sabrina Chmielewski.
